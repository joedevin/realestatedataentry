    Welcome to REMIS

    REMIS stands for the Real Estate Managment Information System. It is a system to help the Realty Specialist stay on top of the various tracts of land that are part of their projects.

    Get Started

    • The left side list contains the tracts entered into the system If you would like to see more information on specific tract type the tract number and click Info

    • Looking at the tract information you can see a checklist of steps for the acquisition.

    • Clicking each step will mark it as complete, clicking again will mark it incomplete

    • Click save to save the information to your Local Storage

    • Click on the New button to create a new tract

    • Click save after entering the information to save the tract to local storage and to add it to the tract list

    Information

    • Tract Number is the tract number assigned by GIS
    • Owner Name is the full name of the current landowner
    • Owner Address is the current mailing address for the landowner
    • APN is the Acessor's Parcel Number for the property being acquired
    • Owner Phone Number is the best phone number to reach the landowner
